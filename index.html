CrystalsCollector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. Think blackjack!

Each crystal has its own unique and hidden value. Each time the game starts, the values of the crystals all change.
